Simplyfied FCKeditor for Calendar-Plugin based on
normal FCKeditor from CMSimple_XH v 1.4.

The standard FCKeditor from CMSimple v1.4 works also, 
except \plugins\calendar\fckeditor\FCKeditor\editor\plugins\sitelink\sitelink.php, 
where some slight adjustments are required (for details see in the file).
